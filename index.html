About

XXXTENTACION

Jahseh Dwayne Ricardo Onfroy

2013 - 2018

The album 17 was released in 2017, as a debut studio album, by labels Bad Vibes
Forever and Empire Distribution.

XXXTentacion stated that 17 targets everyone who suffers from depression, with
the goal of curing or, at least, numb it.

Features multiple genres: emo rap, lo-fi, alternative rock, R&B.
